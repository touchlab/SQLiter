Encrypted Databases​

We recommend using SQLCipher for building encrypted databases. Be aware that Apple (depending on OS version) may, or may not, also be building against the SEE (SQLite Encryption Extension). Take a look at the Touchlab blog post written by Sam Hill that goes into a lot more details. The follow-up post by Kevin Galligan clarifies a couple more details.

See:

  • https://www.zetetic.net/sqlcipher/
  • https://dev.to/touchlab/multiplatform-encryption-with-sqldelight-and-sqlcipher-5do4
  • https://medium.com/@kpgalligan/sqlcipher-and-kmm-58d96ea8095d
